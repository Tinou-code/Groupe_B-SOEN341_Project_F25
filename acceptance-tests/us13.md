## This acceptance test is for the following user story and feature

#### US.13 As an administrator, I want to manage organizations and assign roles, so that platform governance and permissions are properly maintained

## Steps and expected results 
1. Login as an admin. You can login with the following info:
```
Admin id : A12345
password: 12345
``` 
2. Click on ```Manage organizations``` in the sidebar - this will allow you to see all the club. Once you choose a club, you will see all club members and their personal informations (ID, Name, Role, Account status). You will also see the events that the clubs are organizing (Event ID, Title, Date, Location, Capacity, Remaining Tickets, Event status). Then you can click on the dropdown list and assign a role to the members: Owner, Organizer and Staff/Moderator

#### Expected results
- Once clicking on the club you will see all people info and their role
- Once clicking on the dropdown menu you can change the role of someone and it will affects the status of that member.
